Frequently Asked Questions

What is the porta potty rental cost per day?

The cost of a porta potty varies based on the unit type and rental duration. A standard unit has a predictable porta potty rental cost per day, while a luxury porta potty rental cost reflects the premium amenities. We provide fast, transparent quotes tailored to your needs.

Can portable toilets be placed on uneven terrain or slopes?

Ideally, portable toilets should be placed on a flat, stable, and level surface to ensure stability and prevent tipping, especially when in use. While minor variations in terrain can sometimes be accommodated with leveling blocks, significant slopes or uneven ground are not suitable for safe placement. Placing units on unstable surfaces can pose a safety risk to users and make servicing difficult. If your site has challenging terrain, please discuss this with our team during the consultation phase. We may be able to suggest alternative placement locations or specialized solutions to ensure safe and effective deployment.

What happens if the unit is vandalized during my rental?

You are generally responsible for the unit while it is in your possession. However, we offer optional damage waivers for peace of mind. If vandalism occurs, call us immediately to secure the area and arrange for a replacement.

How do you ensure the units stay clean during a long event?

Prevention is key. We help you rent the correct number of units for your crowd size. For multi-day events, we schedule proactive servicing (pumping, cleaning, and restocking) to maintain hygiene standards.

I'm worried about a porta potty at my wedding. What are my options?

A standard porta potty at wedding events is a major risk to the guest experience. We highly recommend our luxury portable restroom trailer, which functions and feels like an upscale indoor bathroom, eliminating that risk entirely.
